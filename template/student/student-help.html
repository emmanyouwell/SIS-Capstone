Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address or LRN, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password.
How do I view my grades?
Navigate to the "Grades" section from the sidebar. You can view your grades by subject and grading period. Grades are updated by your teachers throughout the school year.
How can I enroll for the next school year?
Go to the "Enrollment" section and click on "Online Enrollment Form". Fill out all required information and submit the form. You'll receive a confirmation once your enrollment is processed.
How do I check my class schedule?
Access the "Schedule" section from the sidebar to view your daily class schedule, including subjects, time slots, and room assignments. Your schedule is updated at the beginning of each semester.
How can I contact my teachers?
Use the "Messages" section to send messages to your teachers. You can also check the "Announcements" section for important updates from your teachers and the school administration.
